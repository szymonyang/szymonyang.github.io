My work

As a part of UNSW Computer Science program (COMP2121 course), in a team of 2 we have written an Assembly code to simulate the microwave on a board.Built from scratch to well-functioned product, we quite enjoy it and learn a lot.

Web development and design is one of my most enjoyable things in my spare time. I used Django framework in Python and make some mock-websites.
